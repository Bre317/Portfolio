The Case of the Cookie Company

My data journey began with butter, sugar, and a simple question: "How many of my cookie customers are coming back?" While helping with a small cookie business, I realized we had names, orders, and dates - but no clear picture of customer loyalty.

I dove into the data and discovered something exciting: 40% of customers were repeat buyers, but they weren't being recognized or rewarded. By creating a simple customer segmentation, we designed targeted coupon campaigns that increased repeat purchases by 25% in just two months.

That's when it clicked: Data isn't just numbers - it's the secret story of your business waiting to be told.
