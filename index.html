Hi, I’m Breanna — but you can call me Bre.

I graduated from the University of Arizona (go Wildcats!) with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Computing, with an emphasis in Information Management. I’ve always been naturally curious, and I tend to approach problems like puzzles. I like asking questions, figuring things out on my own, and really understanding how something works instead of just getting to the answer.

I thrive in structured environments that still allow for some independence. I enjoy having clear expectations while also being trusted to manage my work and think through problems on my own. I originally started out as a software development major, but I realized I missed the visual side of things — the “pretty colors,” as I like to say. Applied Computing gave me a better balance between code, data, and design, which felt like a much better fit for how I think and work.

Outside of work and school, I love traveling and exploring new cities, and spending time with my family. We’re pretty close-knit, and that sense of connection and support is something I really value and try to bring into the way I work with others.
